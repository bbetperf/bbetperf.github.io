## What is FUI?

Fictional User Interface — the screens, dashboards, and displays designed for movies and TV. They are never meant to be used by real people, which makes them uniquely free.

## Why it matters?

FUI designers work backwards from emotion. They ask "what should this *feel* like?" before asking "what does this *do*?". That inversion is worth borrowing.

## Patterns worth stealing.

- High contrast at the cost of readability (legibility isn't always the goal)
- Animation as narrative, not feedback
- Colour as system state, not decoration
