Mesoki

[meh-soh-kee]

Since summer 2023, I have been leading the development of Mesoki, a brand built around its signature product-minimalistic MagSafe iPhone cases. These back-cover-only designs, featuring a sleek, matte finish without side bumpers, offer a distinctive aesthetic and functionality. Mesoki's first Instagram post launched on November 3, 2023, ahead of similar entries from competitors Suti (November 26, 2023) and Nomad (February 13, 2024). This innovative idea, which originated on December 12, 2020, has been central to shaping the brand's identity.

About

Mesoki is a brand focused on creating minimalist MagSafe iPhone cases with a sleek, back-cover-only design. These matte, plastic cases offer a refined look without side bumpers, combining functionality with a modern aesthetic. Mesoki’s concept is rooted in simplicity and innovation, standing out in the market by launching ahead of competitors like Suti and Nomad. Crafted with precision, these cases redefine everyday iPhone protection while staying true to the brand’s core vision of minimalism and style.

Colors

Pink, Green, Blue, White, Orange, and Magenta. each shade brings a vibrant touch to your iPhone, combining style with a sleek, matte finish.

Videos

Mesoki’s videos are a fusion of creativity and precision, created using Blender and After Effects. I used freely available sound effects to enhance the viewing experience. Advanced expressions and carefully crafted materials and models based on precisely designed 3D printed patterns were an integral part of the production. Each video s the sleek back design of our MagSafe iPhone cases, while showcasing their innovative features and vibrant colors.

Design

Mesoki MagSafe iPhone cases embody a proportional, minimalist design, featuring a clean back structure with a matte finish. Created through a meticulous design process, each case is engineered for balance and elegance, delivering both style and functionality. My streamlined approach s the essence of minimalism, combining visual appeal with practical sophistication.

Morse

↑ Video above

In the Morse video, the Mesoki brand comes to life through a unique sensory experience. Using After Effects, I transformed the Mesoki name into a series of subtle vibrations that mimic Morse code, creating a rhythmic pulse that resonates with the viewer. Combined with a custom sound design, these vibrations enhance the immersion, building anticipation and excitement with each pulse. The result is an immersive experience that leaves a strong human impression.

Next

As Mesoki continues to evolve, I am focused on taking the next step: securing investors to bring our vision to a wider market. After months of refining my product, designing every detail with precision, and creating captivating visuals I am now preparing to scale. My mission is to expand Mesoki’s presence and introduce our innovative minimalist designs to a global audience. With a solid foundation in place, I am ready for the next chapter - partnering with those who believe in the future of sleek, functional tech accessories.
