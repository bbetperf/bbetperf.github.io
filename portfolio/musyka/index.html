Musyka

[moo-zee-ka]

In 2024, I created a personal project, focusing on process and product design. I independently developed and implemented key features, refining the interface and optimizing the user experience. Below are s of the work, showcasing interface innovation, seamless feature integration, and thoughtful design solutions.

Control Center

On the Control Center screen of the music app, key interaction elements are presented, including the track's cover art, playback controls (play, pause, skip), a progress bar for navigating through the track, and the track's details like title and artist. A notable feature is the visible user comment with a time-stamped reference, allowing social interaction around specific moments in the track. Additional controls for managing favorites, shuffle, repeat, and playlist access are neatly organized at the bottom, ensuring a clean and intuitive user experience.

Explore Page

On the home screen of the music app, key interaction elements are presented, including the Explorety feature for discovering an endless stream of personalized music, a search bar for finding new content, and user profile access. Below, the "For You" section offers curated recommendations through visually distinct music cards. A bottom navigation bar provides quick access to Explore, Library, and Friends, ensuring smooth navigation and a focused user experience.

Friends Page

On the friends screen of the music app, key interaction elements are presented, including a search bar for finding friends and feature cards for SharePlay, Chats, Updates, and Comments, ing social activity.
