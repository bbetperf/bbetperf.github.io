Noque

[nok-yoo]

is a mobile marketplace app designed for restaurants and cafes. It streamlines the dining experience by allowing customers to browse menus, place orders, and enjoy loyalty programs from multiple establishments all in one place. Tailored for both small and large businesses, Noque provides a cost-effective solution for enhancing customer engagement and simplifying operations.

Goals

The goal of the project was to simplify the ordering process by integrating multiple restaurants and cafes into one app, making it more convenient for customers. It aimed to provide an affordable platform for small and medium-sized restaurants to offer loyalty programs and promotions without needing individual apps. Additionally, the project sought to foster customer loyalty through personalized offers and a smooth interface, while helping restaurants expand their reach and increase visibility by joining a centralized marketplace. Ultimately, the project transitioned from a concept to a scalable business model with the potential to attract investment and partnerships.

Role and tasks

I originated and developed the concept for an app designed to enhance interactions between restaurants and their customers. My responsibilities included creating an intuitive, engaging interface with a focus on user experience and animations to provide smooth, memorable interactions. As art director, I managed the visual and design aspects, ensuring consistency between aesthetics and functionality. I also led a team of developers, analysts, and sales managers, fostering collaboration to achieve the project's goals. Additionally, I transformed the concept into a scalable business model aimed at attracting investment and promoting growth.
